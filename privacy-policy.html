Privacy Policy

TrayForm Pro — by Nexel · Last updated: March 2026

TrayForm Pro ("the App") is a cable tray cutting template tool developed by Nexel. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Data Collection

TrayForm Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App works entirely offline on your device. No account, registration, or login is required.

2. Data Usage

All calculations and measurements entered into the App (width, height, angle, etc.) are processed locally on your device and are never sent to any server. No usage analytics or tracking tools are used.

3. Internet Access

The App may request internet access solely to load the initial web application from GitHub Pages (nexel-app.github.io/trayform-pro). Once loaded, it functions fully offline via a service worker cache.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

TrayForm Pro is a professional tool intended for adults working in electrical and construction trades. It is not directed at children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@nexel.app
